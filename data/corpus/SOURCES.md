# Corpus Source List

This directory contains reference materials used for grounding the MedNote Scribe AI agent. The materials are divided into ICD-10-CM diagnosis codes and clinical documentation guidelines.

---

## 1. ICD-10-CM Code Reference Subset
- **Source**: Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC) / National Center for Health Statistics (NCHS)
- **Database/Edition**: ICD-10-CM (Clinical Modification), Fiscal Year 2026 Release
- **Content Covered**: 
  - Headache syndromes (R51.9, G44.2, G44.209, G44.219, G44.229)
  - Migraine syndromes (G43.909)
  - Cardiovascular conditions and symptoms (R07.9, I21.9, I10)
  - Endocrine and metabolic conditions (E11.9)
  - Genitourinary conditions (N39.0)
  - Acute lower respiratory infections (J20.9)
  - Injuries to the ankle and foot (S93.401A, S93.402A, S93.409A)
- **URL**: [https://www.cdc.gov/nchs/icd/icd10cm.htm](https://www.cdc.gov/nchs/icd/icd10cm.htm)

---

## 2. Clinical Documentation Guidelines
- **Source**: Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services (CMS) / American Medical Association (AMA)
- **Guidelines**: 2023 Evaluation and Management (E/M) Services Guidelines and SOAP format documentation standards.
- **Content Covered**:
  - Core structures of SOAP formatting.
  - Scribe compliance standards (Joint Commission and CMS rules on scribe documentation).
  - Medical risk management criteria for urgent clinical conditions (Red Flags).
- **URL**: [https://www.cms.gov/medicare/payment/fee-for-service-providers/physician-fee-schedule/evaluation-and-management](https://www.cms.gov/medicare/payment/fee-for-service-providers/physician-fee-schedule/evaluation-and-management)
